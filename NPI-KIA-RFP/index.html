WHO WE ARE

Northern Productions Inc. (NPI) was born in July of 1987 (34 years) and has been operating successfully since its inception.

We have grown into a proudly Canadian full-service display company specializing in Custom Exhibits, Interior Environments, Graphic Production and Experiential Marketing.

Built on a solid foundation of leadership & experience; NPI has successfully personalized our services with our client's unique needs nationally and internationally.
